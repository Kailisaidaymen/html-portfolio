The Best Movies According To Me

My Best Movies Of All-Time.

The Godfather

A mafia masterpiece. Sharp writing, flawless acting, and a story that builds real tension. It’s the blueprint for crime dramas

The Godfather 2

A rare sequel that matches the original. Deeper, darker, and shows the rise of Vito vs. the fall of Michael. Pure cinematic peak.

The Godfather 3

Not on the same level, but still solid. It closes Michael’s story with guilt, power, and consequences. Underrated if you watch it with the right expectations.
